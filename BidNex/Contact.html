Help - Online Auction Platform

Creating an Account

To participate in auctions, you need to create an account. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Home Page.
  2. Click on "Sign Up" or "Register".
  3. Fill in the required information (name, email, password).
  4. Click "Submit" or "Create Account".
  5. You will receive a confirmation email. Click the link to activate your account.

Placing a Bid

To bid on an item, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Find the item you want to bid on.
  3. Enter your bid amount in the designated field.
  4. Click "Place Bid".
  5. If your bid is the highest when the auction ends, you win!

FAQs

How do I pay for items I win?

Payment methods and procedures vary. Generally, you'll receive instructions after winning an auction.

Can I retract a bid?

Unfortunately, once placed, bids cannot be retracted. Please bid carefully.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the contact form on our website or via email at support@example.com.
